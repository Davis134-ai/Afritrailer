MTN MoMo Instructions

  1. Click the "Confirm Recharge" button below.
  2. Wait 1–2 minutes for a USSD prompt to appear on your phone with the phone number used to register your AfriTrailer account.
  3. You'll receive a prompt asking you to enter your 5-digit MoMo PIN to confirm the withdrawal.
  4. Enter your PIN and the recharge will immediately reflect in your account wallet.

Airtel Money Instructions

  1. Dial *185# on your Airtel line to open the Airtel Money menu.
  2. Choose the option for "Withdraw Cash".
  3. Enter the amount you'd like to withdraw.
  4. Once completed, put the secret code given to you in the input below and click "Confirm Recharge".
  5. Recharge will immediately reflect in your account wallet.
